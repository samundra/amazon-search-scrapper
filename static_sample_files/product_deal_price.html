4.0 out of 5 starsYou get what you pay for.
By T. Knight on March 14, 2013
Verified Purchase
While not a terrible product, there are very obvious quality differences between this product and well known brands (e.g. Sylvania Mosaic). For the purposes of this review, I will be comparing these LEDs to Sylvania's Mosaic product which I also own.

Pros: Inexpensive, bright, Good remote & controller, decent (but cheap) power supply.

Cons: The smell, poor white color rendering, terrible pvc water proofing.

The Smell: The waterproofing on the LEDs is either an unstable chemical mixture, or simply not cured. It is obviously outgassing, as the smell is incredibly over-powering, like plumbing PVC solvent. The smell even transfers to your hands if you handle it. When left in my bathroom, the whole room will smelled like a plastic/rubber factory in about 15 minutes even with the exhaust fan running. The clear coating feels tacky, somewhat like the adhesive on a Post-it. The coating is quite soft compared to Mosaic's strip, and there is evidence of rippling in the first few feet of the coating when I started to unreel the LEDs. The rippling is of concern because it disperses the light when directly over an LED. I allowed the reel to warm up a bit in an attempt to complete the curing, but all it did was make the room smell worse. I've read from another reviewer that he put his in the oven to finish it; I will not be doing that. UV has a tendency to harden plastics, so I will try leaving them out in the sun a while. Hopefully this will at least mitigate the smell if it doesn't harden it.

Further, the waterproof coating on this product is not quite 'clear' when compared to Sylvania's. The Mosaic LED's waterproofing looks as clear as glass, feels solid and is not in anyway sticky. It's exactly what you'd expect from the coating.Read more ›

5.0 out of 5 starsPerfect cabinet lighting for my kitchen
By R. McDonald on October 29, 2013
Verified Purchase
I Purchased four kits along with some connectors and RGB wire and used these to provide lighting in a shadow box, under-cabinet lighting, and toe-kick lighting in my kitchen.

I am so pleased with the product. It was easy to install and use and is easy to cut and reconnect using quick connectors. The sticky stuff on the back worked pretty well for holding it on regular wall paint and non shiny but smooth surfaces. I had a small bit of trouble with sticking on high gloss painted surfaces or surfaces that were rough, but tiny dots of superglue solved that problem.

I usually have the lights set to a custom color, a rather dim, warm white. When they are on in full bright white mode they are VERY bright. These strips have MANY more LEDs per inch than most of the brands I have been seeing in the large home improvement stores and most of what I see there either is not really as flexible as this is or requires chaining together many tiny 12 inch long pieces just to span a length like a 39 inch cabinet.

I plan to buy several more kits for other projects in other rooms.

The only thing about the lights that bothered me is that the default for the controller when you first plug in is a particularly aggressive flashing display of 7 colors. Quite a surprise the first time.

5.0 out of 5 starsGreat LED Light KIT with just a few issues.
By MardeeT on August 31, 2014
It's not a Phillip's Hue Strip, but it does have some nice lighting and features. You can cut these at a weld point every three LEDs for angles and all, you just have to mend it back together to keep the current flowing. These are listed as waterproof and can be used out doors. They have a really strong smell to them out of the box, probably some weather proofing coating, so if you use it indoors be prepared for a stink for awhile. Also the 3M tape on the back was not very sticky. I tried to stick it to my wood desk frame but it kept coming undone. Very frustrating. Overall though it produces a really nice light effect, so many choices on the 44key remote, dim, brighten, flash for a strobe effect. Alternate all the colors quickly, control the speed of it. You have to be sure to keep the IR receiver fairly outward facing or you will have remote issues. 70 Watts of power make these pretty darn bright. I also like the DIY keys to program your own little effect show and play it back. This would be a five star if not for the smell and the poor sticky tape on it. It adds a nice light aura to the wall behind my work desk and I control the color to whatever my mood and music taste is at the moment. TaoTronics provided me a sample product to review, and did not ask me to write favorable information, etc. This is a fair and honest review.

Update: Several Days later....
The smell has went away after using them on high power for a bit. Also I changed where I stuck them to my wood desk, which was originally the lip edge under the desk top. I just stuck them to the desk under this all the way around and they seem to be sticking better there. My original placement must not have had them where they were getting fixed well. So with these issues gone, I have bumped it from 4 stars to 5 stars. I really like them! Make my desk work area so much nicer.
